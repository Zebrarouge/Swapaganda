Swapaganda Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Swapaganda is a Chrome extension that does not collect, transmit, or store any personally‑identifying information or browsing data off your device. All text replacements and user settings are managed locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local.

Data Collection: None. Swapaganda does not send any data to external servers or third parties.

Data Storage: Your custom word‑swap lists and toggle state are stored locally in your browser’s storage and are never shared.

Third‑Party Services: Swapaganda does not integrate with or rely on any third‑party analytics, advertising, or other external services.

For any questions or concerns, please contact zebrarouge.dev@gmail.com.
